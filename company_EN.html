Business Residence Permit (Self-Employed Guest)

Perfect solution for freelancers and individual entrepreneurs who want to conduct business in Hungary and legally reside in the EU. Foreign citizens working independently for remuneration (freelance, services, online activities), or general directors and founders of registered commercial organizations can obtain residence permits.

Learn requirements Get consultation

Main Requirements

Who can apply

Foreign citizens working independently for remuneration (freelance, services, online activities), or general directors and founders of registered commercial organizations.

Validity period

Residence permit — 1 year, with extension up to maximum 3 years. Extension is possible only if you have been in Hungary for at least 90 days in the last 180 days, if you have no reporting delays.

Documents

Income confirmation, housing documents, medical insurance, proof of sufficient means of subsistence, confirmation of residence purpose, photos and biometrics.

Important to know

Limitations

  • Mandatory regular reporting
  • Family reunification impossible during the first year
  • Cannot change residence purpose in Hungary to another
  • Status does not provide rights to national permanent residence

Procedure

  • Documents can be submitted outside Hungary through consulate
  • In Hungary — if entry is visa-free
  • Document processing time — up to 30 days
  • Possibility of obtaining EU long-term resident after 5 years
