Main Requirements

Financial Requirements

  • Minimum income: €2,160 per month
  • Additional €540 for each family member
  • Income proof for the last 3 months

Employer Requirements

  • Registered outside Spain
  • Company exists for at least 1 year
  • Contract for minimum 1 year

Required Documents

  • Passport (valid for 1+ year)
  • Criminal record certificate (apostilled)
  • Medical insurance
  • Employment contract or agreements
