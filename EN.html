Legalization options

Choose your path, and we’ll help you every step of the way!

Here are the most in-demand services — residence permits, permanent residency, and citizenship — with brief descriptions. To learn more and ensure this option suits your needs, contact our specialist.

Digital nomad residence permit | White card
Learn more

Digital nomad residence permit

A residence permit for digital nomads in Hungary is issued for one year with the option to extend for another year. This is an ideal solution for those planning to live in the country while earning income from abroad. Processing time: 7 to 30 days.

Hungary national card residence permit
Learn more

National card

Issued for up to 3 years with the option to renew for another 3 years. After continuous residence under the National Card for 3 years, applicants become eligible for permanent residency in Hungary. Processing time: 30 to 70 days.

Business Residence Permit in Hungary
Learn more

Business Residence Permit

A residence permit for self-employed individuals allows you to legally live in Hungary, run a business, or own a company. It is issued for up to 2 years with the option to renew. After 5 years of residence, you can apply for permanent residency.

Residence Permit for Students
Learn more

Residence Permit for Students and Scholarship Program Participants

A residence permit for studying at a university in Hungary or participating in a scholarship program. Options for paid or free education are available. Processing time: 30 to 60 days.

Tourist | Visitor Visa
Learn more

Tourist | Visitor Visa

A short-term visa issued to citizens or residents of the country where the application is submitted. Suitable for tourism or visiting friends and relatives. Processing time: 7-15 days from the date of submission.

Permanent Residence | Citizenship
Learn more

Permanent Residence | Citizenship

After living in Hungary for 3-5 years, you can apply for permanent residence (PR), and after 5-10 years, for citizenship. These statuses grant you access to all the benefits of living in the EU, including freedom of movement and additional rights.
