Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any obligations related to the minimum duration of stay in Hungary for holders of a guest investor residence permit?

No, holders of a guest investor residence permit are not required to meet a minimum duration of stay in Hungary. Learn more

What taxes need to be paid when applying for a residence permit under the National Card program through company registration in Hungary as a director or specialist?

In 2025, the minimum wage in Hungary is 290,800 HUF (~€700), and the guaranteed minimum wage for skilled workers is 348,800 HUF (~€850). Learn more

What is the amount and procedure for depositing the share capital when opening a company?

The share capital is declared during company registration, but the actual deposit into a bank account can be postponed.
Learn more

Can I obtain sole entrepreneur (self-employed) status in Hungary without a residence permit?

You must first obtain a residence permit
(Hungarian residency) before you can register as a self-employed person (egyéni vállalkozó).

Is a legal address required to register as a sole entrepreneur in Hungary?

Yes, registering as a self-employed person
(egyéni vállalkozó) in Hungary requires a legal address (székhely).
We provide this service!
You can rent a legal address through us—this will help you quickly and easily register as a sole entrepreneur in Hungary.

Can I apply for a residence permit under the National Card program for my entire family (spouse, children) at the same time?

Yes, if the spouse and children are officially registered, they can apply simultaneously with the main applicant. It is important that the family meets the reunification requirements and provides all necessary documents.
