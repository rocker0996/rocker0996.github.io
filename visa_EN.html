Schengen Visa Application

Last change:
March 28, 2025

Applying for a Schengen visa requires strict adherence to requirements, thorough document preparation, and attention to numerous details.
Even a minor mistake can lead to a refusal.
We offer professional support at every stage of the application process, helping you save time and avoid the risk of rejection.

To obtain a Schengen visa, you need to provide a number of mandatory documents, including:
• A valid international passport issued no more than 10 years ago, with at least two blank pages;
• Copies of all visas and official stamps, made according to the rules (scale 1:1, strict formatting);
• Internal passport (for Russian citizens) and correct copies;
• Two photos that meet ICAO standards;
• A completed application form in English or Hungarian — with no mistakes or omissions;
• Tickets and itinerary confirmation, including all travel within the Schengen Area;
• Proof of accommodation — fully paid, verified;
• Medical insurance with coverage of at least 30,000 euros;
• Financial guarantees (bank statements, work certificates, sponsorship, etc.);
• Additional documents related to employment, marital status, etc.;
• For minors — a separate set of documents, including parental consent.
