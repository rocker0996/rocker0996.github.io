Hungary National Card

National Card – Residence Permit through Employer or Business Ownership
The National Card is a residence permit (RP) in Hungary that allows individuals to legally reside and work in the country. This type of RP is available both for employment with an employer and for running a business through your own company. Let’s review the main requirements and nuances of the application process.

1. RP through Employer
If you plan to obtain the National Card through employment, you need to meet the following conditions:

Employment Contract: You must have a valid employment contract with a Hungarian employer.

Income Level: Your salary must be sufficient to cover living expenses and meet the legal requirements.

Employer Documents:
The employer must provide:
• company details,
• confirmation of company registration,
• tax declarations and proof of no debts,
• a letter confirming your employment.

Proof of Qualifications: In some cases, diplomas or certificates confirming your professional qualifications may be required.

2. RP through Business Ownership
If you plan to obtain the National Card by running your own business in Hungary, the following requirements must be met:
• Company Registration: Your company must be registered in Hungary (Kft or Zrt).
• Financial Activity: You need to prove that the company is actively operating and generating financial turnover.
This can be done through:
• bank statements,
• accounting reports,
• contracts with partners and clients.

Company Documents:
Registered Address: The company must have a registered legal address.
Company Income: The company must generate income sufficient to support the owner’s living expenses.
No Debts: Proof of no debts for taxes or other obligations must be provided.

Additional Documents*
Regardless of the application method for the National Card, you will need the following documents:
• A rental agreement or proof of property ownership in Hungary.
• Health insurance valid in Hungary.
• Bank statements proving your financial stability.
