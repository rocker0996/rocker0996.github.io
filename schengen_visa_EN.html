Visa types and basics

Visa C: up to 90/180 days

Short-term trips: tourism, business, visiting, study, treatment, sport. Multiple-entry visa is possible with convincing history and purpose.

Multiple-entry visa 1–5 years

Issued with solid travel history and strong ties to country of residence. Consular decision is individual; proper justification is key.

Information

Who needs a Hungary Schengen visa

  • Nationals of countries requiring Schengen visas (without a valid residence/visa allowing entry).
  • Those entering and mainly staying via Hungary or with a credible route through Hungary.
  • Even with prior visas: for new trip purposes, documents must be updated and re-justified.

Process and key stages

1
Diagnostics and strategy

Trip purpose, risk analysis, optimal application point (consulate/VFS)

2
Appointment and preparation

Bookings, application form, insurance, financial proofs, cover letter

3
Submission and biometrics

Apply at VFS/consulate, provide biometrics, handle possible additional requests

4
Processing and issuance

Typically up to 15 days; complex cases up to 45 days; passport return

Documents required

Mandatory documents

  • Passport + copies of pages, prior visas/stamps.
  • Completed application, data consent, photo 3.5×4.5.
  • Itinerary and bookings (hotels/invitation, tickets or ticket reservations).
  • Insurance covering at least €30,000, valid across the Schengen area.

Finances and justification

  • Proof of income/savings: employer/bank letters, statements.
  • Evidence of ties: family, employment, studies, property.
  • Cover letter explaining trip logic and route.

Typical refusal reasons

  • Doubts about trip purpose or route inconsistent with purpose.
  • Insufficient financial proof or unstable bank statements.
  • Weak ties to country of residence (job/family/property).
  • Returnability doubts, incomplete bookings, inconsistent dates.

How we mitigate risks

  • Submission strategy: consulate selection, route, requested visa duration.
  • Proper package: verifiable bookings, relevant finances, persuasive cover letter.
  • Interview preparation and documentation checklists, handling requests.
  • Appeals and re-submission support if needed.
